Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl

Pomeranian

While the white Pomeranian is rare, there are breeders who have them. Weighing between 3 to 7 pounds, they're nicknamed "the little dog who thinks he can" because they're very active and agile despite their size.

Hamster

They are chunky-bodied, thick-furred, short-tailed rodents with cheek pouches and are secretive burrowers and hoarders. Being nocturnal creatures, they usually sleep during the day and play and explore at night.
